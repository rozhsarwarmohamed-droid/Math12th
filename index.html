🎲 Chance!

Some things will happen, some things won't happen, and some things might happen.

✅

Will Happen

We are certain it will happen. It cannot miss!

❌

Won't Happen

It is impossible. There is no way it can happen.

🤔

Might Happen

We are not sure. It could happen or it might not!

📊 The Chance Scale

❌ Impossible🤔 Might Happen✅ Certain

Chance goes from impossible all the way to certain. Most events fall somewhere in between!

💡 Did you know? Knowing about chance helps us make better decisions! If you know it might rain, you bring an umbrella ☔

🧩 Worked Example

Let's work through this together — step by step!

📚 Worked Example 1
You have six dominoes lying face down on the table.
What is the chance you will turn over a domino with a total of six spots?
Will it happen? Won't it happen? Or might it happen?

The dominoes are face DOWN — we cannot see the spots!

🤔 Think: We cannot see the faces of the dominoes. We don't know how many spots each one has. Can we be certain there is one with six spots?
🎯 Answer: Might happen!
Because the dominoes are face down, we cannot see their spots. There might be a domino with 6 spots — or there might not. We cannot be certain either way!
👉 Key rule: If we cannot see the items, the answer is usually "might happen."
💡 Remember: Hidden items → uncertain outcome → "Might happen." When you CAN see all the items, you can make a more certain judgement!
